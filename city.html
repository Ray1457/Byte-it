THE CYBER TOWER

Navi Mumbai’s landmarks, like the imposing Cyber Tower, stand as grim reminders of the city’s technological excess. These structures, while impressive, are also epicenters for cyberpsychosis outbreaks, triggered by the relentless digital overload.

SKYLINE OF DESPAIR

The skyline of Navi Mumbai is dominated by towering, neon-lit skyscrapers that once symbolized progress but now cast long shadows of despair. Digital billboards flicker with ominous advertisements, reflecting the city's decaying state and the pervasive threat of cyberpsychosis
